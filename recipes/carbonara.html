Carbonara

Description

Pasta carbonara is a simple but delicious dish

Ingredients

  • Egg yolks
  • Pancetta
  • Parmesan
  • Onion
  • Pasta
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Stir egg yolks with salt, pepper and parmesan.
  2. Put pan fill with water to boil and add pinch of salt.
  3. Meanwhile, cut large slices of pencetta and fry them lightly with olive oil.
  4. When the boil add the pasta.
  5. Take 2 spoon of cooking water and add them with the egg yolks and mix
  6. Set aside the pencetta and keep the frying pan warm.
  7. when the pasta are Al dente drain them and put them on the frying pan and stir them up
  8. Add the mix in it and stir.
  9. Finally add the pancetta
