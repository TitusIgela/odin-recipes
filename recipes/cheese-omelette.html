Cheese Omelette

Homepage

Description

Easy meal to start the day !

Ready in 5 minutes with just a couples of ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Break the eggs in a bowl.
  2. Stir the eggs.
  3. Melt butter in a hot frying pan.
  4. Add the mixed eggs and let settle.
  5. Add mozzarella on top and fold it in half
  6. Season with salt and pepper.
  7. Let it cool down before serving.
