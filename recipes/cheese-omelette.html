Cheese omelette

Description

This cheese omelette is perfect for breakfast. When cooking an omelette, it's important that you keep the temperature on the low side, as you don;t want it to turn brown.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon milk
  • salt and pepper
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1/4 cup Shredded Emmentaler cheese

Steps

  1. whisk eggs in a bowl; add milk and season with salt and pepper. whisk until egg mixture is foamy.
  2. Melt butter in a small, nonstick skillet over medium-low heat. Pour in egg mixture and swirl the skillet, so the bottom is evenly covered with egg. Cook until egg starts to set. Lift edges with a spatula and tilt the skillet so uncooked egg can flow towards the bottom of the skillet to set. Repeat until no visible liquid egg remains.
  3. Carefully flip omelette; cook until warmed through. Sprinkle Emmentaler cheese down the middle; fold in half. Cook until cheese os melted. Slide omelette onto a plate.
