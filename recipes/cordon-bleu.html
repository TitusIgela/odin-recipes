Cordon Bleu

Homepage

Description

Favorite kids meal.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Ham
  • Eggs yolk
  • Salt/pepper
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Cheese
  • Flour

Steps

  1. Cut some slim slice of chicken breast.
  2. Flatten them with a food-hammer.
  3. Put a slice of ham on top of it.
  4. Add some cheese on top and fold it in half.
  5. Dip it in the eggs yolk then in flour.
  6. Dip it again in the yolk but now use the breadcrumbs to make the crust
  7. Deep fry it and serve it hot
