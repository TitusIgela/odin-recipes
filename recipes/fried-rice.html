Fried rice

Description

Fried rice Asian dishes.

Ingredients

  • White rice
  • Soy sauce
  • Peas
  • Eggs
  • Tofu
  • Cooking oil
  • Salt
  • Carrot
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Cook the rice
  2. Cut Onion and carrot
  3. Fry them lightly with oil
  4. Add the peas and the tofu
  5. Add the cook rice and stir
  6. Add the eggs and let it cook for a bit
  7. Pour some soy sauce and stir well
  8. Season to your liking, it's ready !
