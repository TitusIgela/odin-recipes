Omelette

Description

Easy to make yummy to eat

Ingredients

  • Grated cheese
  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cooking oil

Steps

  1. Break the shell and mix yolks and white in a bowl
  2. In The hot frying pan pour some oil
  3. Add the mix
  4. Put cheese on top
  5. Fold the omelette
  6. Season
  7. Et voila !
