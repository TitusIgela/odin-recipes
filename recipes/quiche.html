Quiche

Homepage

Description

Easy dish for picnic

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Dough
  • Cheese
  • Salt/pepper
  • Bacon
  • Sour cream
  • Curry

Steps

  1. Put the dough in a pie dish then prick it.
  2. Mix eggs, sour cream,curry, salt and pepper .
  3. Cut small bit of bacon and fry it.
  4. Pour the mix on the dough and add the bacon.
  5. Add cheese on top.
  6. Put it in the oven for one hour
  7. Serve it hot or cold
