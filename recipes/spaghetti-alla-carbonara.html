Spaghetti alla carbonara

Description

This classic spaghetti alla carbonara recipe is smooth, creamy, and full of rich Italian flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 500g guanciale, diced
  • 500g spaghetti
  • 3 large eggs
  • 50g grated Percorino Romano cheese, divided
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat; add guanciale. Cook, turning occasionally, until evenly browned and crispy, 5 to 10 minutes. Remove from heat and drain on paper towels.
  2. Bring a large pat of salted water to a boil. Cook spaghetti in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until tender yet firm to bite, about 9 minutes, Drain and return to the pot. Let cool, stirring occasionally , about 5 minutes
  3. Whisk eggs, half of the Percorino Romano cheese, and some black pepper in a bowl until smooth an creamy
  4. Pour egg mixture over pasta, stirring quickly, until creamy and slightly cooled. Stir in guanciale
  5. Top with remaining Percorino Romano cheese amd more black pepper.
